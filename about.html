About Daniel Lamptey

Daniel Lamptey's journey into the world of circus arts began with humble beginnings. Despite his parents' initial disapproval, Daniel pursued his passion with relentless determination. His breakthrough came with his first invitation to perform at Halikarnas, The Club, in Turkey.

Since then, Daniel has captivated audiences around the globe with his exceptional talent and extraordinary performances.

More About Daniel

Daniel Lamptey is not just a performer, but a storyteller who uses the art of the circus to weave captivating narratives. With over a decade of experience, he has mastered various disciplines including aerial acrobatics, juggling, dish pan spinning and fire eating.

His journey started in his hometown, where he was fascinated by the local street performers. Despite numerous challenges, Daniel's perseverance paid off when he received a scholarship to a prestigious circus school. He graduated top of his class and has since performed in numerous international festivals and shows.
